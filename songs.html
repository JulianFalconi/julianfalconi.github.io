LYRICS


Thursday, 6 September 2018

STRAY

If I gave you my name,
would you give me a call?
But, first, forget about it all
and just feel the fall.
Just feel the fall.

We're all passengers
of the head and the heart;
mine are floating down the river
fishing for ideas to fill.
Oh, I'm floating down the river
fishing for ideas to fill.

Some may think I'm
pushing up a rock,
but I feel more like a stray dog
soaking sun up at the dock.
Oh, I might as well be a stray dog
soaking sun up at the dock.
Oh, freedom is a stray dog
soaking sun up at the dock.
