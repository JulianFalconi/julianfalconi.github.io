Thursday, 30 August 2018

TODAY

Livings wait to be sold -
we have forgotten how to forge.
Cashing living for livings, we go searching
for roots that may withstand those indifferent gasps of somber society.
Circumstantially, we fix on lots to erect ourselves upon
as if Probability didn’t forge from something close to nothing.

Mother Nature,
daughter of Time,
breathes before us:
a cloud ends to begin anew,
a stain rejoices at an excavated smile,
and a steady breeze ventures to embrace folds in space,
each, exhaling moored fragments of ever-flowing wisdom.

A tree,
like a period of a forgotten verse, is someone and anyone
where God’s pen planted an end to a sentence of earth,
like the magnitude of all pasts
is today.


PAINTING

The canvas is worked brown
within a frame of green,

sloping veins are etched to be darkened
by glistening voyages of domesticated water,

and paint is buried

to be rendered by strokes of life
and those who know sustenance
in color.
